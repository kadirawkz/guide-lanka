Dunhinda Waterfalls

Dunhinda Falls is a waterfall located about 5 kilometres from Badulla in the lower central hills of Sri Lanka. Cascading 64 meters into a large pool, it's considered one of the most enchanting falls in the country, named for the smoky dew drops spray that surrounds it.

Muthiyanganaya Temple

Regarded as one of the 16 sacred places in Sri Lanka (Solosmasthana), this ancient temple is believed to have been visited by Lord Buddha. It houses sacred relics enshrined in its majestic stupa, a site of deep spiritual significance.

VISIT TEMPLE

Nine Arches Bridge

Built in 1921, the Nine Arch Bridge is a masterpiece of colonial-era engineering. Spanning 300 feet through dense jungle and tea estates, it's one of the most photographed railway bridges in the world.
