Hakgala Botanical Garden

Situated 5,400 feet above sea level, this garden features a cool temperate climate and over 10,000 species of flora. Famous for its Orchids and Roses, it's a must-visit during the Nuwara Eliya spring season.

VISIT GARDEN

Horton Plains

A national park in the central highlands with montane grassland and cloud forest. Rich in biodiversity and the headwaters of three major rivers, it's a UNESCO World Heritage site.

EXPLORE PLAINS

Adisham Bungalow

A Tudor-style country house built in 1931, Adisham Hall was the home of Sir Thomas Villiers. Today, it serves as a Benedictine monastery and is famous for its beautiful gardens and homemade preserves.

VIEW BUNGALOW

Dunhinda Waterfalls

One of Sri Lanka's most enchanting waterfalls, Dunhinda cascades 64 meters into a large pool, creating a smoky spray that gives it its name. Located just 5km from Badulla town.

SEE FALLS

Muthiyanganaya Temple

An ancient Buddhist temple in Badulla, regarded as one of the 16 sacred places in the country. It is believed to have been visited by Lord Buddha during his third visit to the island.

VISIT TEMPLE

Nine Arches Bridge

The 'Bridge in the Sky' is a colonial-era railway masterpiece between Ella and Demodara. Standing 100 feet tall, it offers spectacular views of rolling hills and dense jungle.

WALK BRIDGE
